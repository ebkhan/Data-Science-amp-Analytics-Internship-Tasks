Task objective:
Predict the likelihood of a loan default and optimize the decision threshold based on cost-
benefit analysis.
Approach:
1)Cleaned and preprocessed the dataset (handled missing values, encoding)

2)Performed feature engineering (e.g., age, employment data)

3)Trained classification models (Logistic Regression)

4)Predicted default probabilities

5)Defined business costs for false positives and false negatives

6)Optimized the classification threshold to minimize total cost

Results & Findings:

1)Optimal threshold is lower than 0.5, reducing risky approvals

2)Cost-based optimization significantly reduced financial loss

3)Key predictors: credit score (EXT_SOURCE), income, and credit amount

4)Model helps improve risk-based decision making
